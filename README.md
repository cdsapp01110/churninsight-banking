# ChurnInsight Banking

Banking customer churn analysis dashboard built to help teams understand revenue loss, identify at-risk customers, explore churn drivers, and prioritize retention strategies.

**Repository:** [cdsapp01110/churninsight-banking](https://github.com/cdsapp01110/churninsight-banking)  
**App Name:** ChurnInsight Banking  
**Live Site:** https://churnbankinganalysis.netlify.app

---

## Overview

**ChurnInsight Banking** is a banking churn analysis dashboard that helps users understand customer churn through revenue impact, customer risk segmentation, churn driver analysis, and retention strategy planning.

The app is designed to answer key business questions such as:

- How much revenue is being lost to churn?
- Which customers are most at risk?
- What behaviors are strongest predictors of churn?
- How does market context affect customer retention?
- What retention actions should be prioritized next?

---

## Features

- **Revenue Impact Analysis**  
  Understand churn through key metrics such as Gross Revenue Retention (GRR), Net Revenue Retention (NRR), monthly revenue loss, and MRR bridge analysis.

- **Customer Risk Segmentation**  
  Classify customers into Low Risk, Medium Risk, High Risk, and Critical Risk groups.

- **Churn Driver Insights**  
  Identify the strongest churn indicators, including product ownership, digital engagement, account balance, and direct deposit behavior.

- **Cohort Retention Analysis**  
  Track retention performance over time and identify weak cohorts or drop-off points.

- **Market Context Dashboard**  
  Explore external banking trends and Charlotte market dynamics that influence churn.

- **Customer Search and Filtering**  
  Search and filter customer-level data by risk, segment, balance, and churn probability.

- **Retention Strategy Recommendations**  
  Translate churn signals into practical action plans and prioritized retention interventions.
